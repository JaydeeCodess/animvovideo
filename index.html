How It Works

Our Production Process

Every video we make follows the same battle-tested 9-step workflow — ensuring quality, speed, and zero surprises.

01

Briefing

We will make your requirements outline and discuss the animation style, mission, and length of your explainer video.

02

Script

Our team will create a script that will enable you to tell a compelling story about your business, product or service.

03

Storyboard

At this stage, our digital artists will prepare a simplified visual representation of how the final explainer video will unfold.

04

Illustrations

Our graphic designers will develop a unique visual style and convert the storyboard into beautiful illustrations based on your brand theme.

05

Voice Over

We collaborate with talented voice artists that can provide a powerful voice for your video. Choose any voice, gender, and language.

06

Animation

We breathe life into static illustrations, bringing together the script, illustrations, and voiceover into an exciting visual story.

07

Background Music

We purchase music from any paid music website based on the video type and style, mix it with the voice over and add to the video.

08

Sound Effects

At this stage we add sound effects / SFX on different actions in the video, to enhance video quality and grab audience attention.

09

Export

We export final video in MP4 format with full HD resolution. We also provide a vertical version for social media platforms.

FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

What types of videos do you produce?+
We produce SaaS explainer videos, product demo videos, 2D explainer videos, motion graphics, UI animations, walkthrough videos, whiteboard videos, logo animations, and social media video ads.
How long does production take?+
Most projects are delivered within 7–14 days. Longer videos may take 15–20 days. Need it faster? We've sprinted before — just let us know.
How much does a video cost?+
Pricing depends on the style, length, and complexity. Book a free call and we'll give you a transparent estimate within 24 hours — no hidden costs.
How many revisions do you offer?+
Unlimited revisions. We share storyboards and style frames early so there are never any big surprises at the end.
What file formats do you deliver?+
Full HD or 4K in MP4, AVI, MOV, WEBM, WMV, GIF, Lottie/JSON, and HTML. We also include a vertical version for social platforms.
Do you work with early-stage startups?+
Absolutely. Solo founders, early-stage startups, and funded growth teams alike. If your message matters — we're in.
